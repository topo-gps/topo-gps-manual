Notifications
=============

In the Notifications section of the settings, you can manage the system alerts sent by the Topo GPS app. Currently, there is only one toggle available: Deviate from route warning. When enabled, the app will alert you if you stray from a route you are following. Please note that this feature requires you to :ref:`record <sec-route-record>` your route.

Once you have loaded a route on the map, if you deviate from the path by 25 meters or more, Topo GPS will trigger an audible alert—even if the app is running in the background. This helpful warning ensures you are quickly notified if you start heading in an unintended direction.
