Notifications
=============

In the section 'Notifications' of the settings you can control the system notifications send by the Topo GPS app.

Currently there is only one switch, 'Deviate from route warning'. If you enable this option, an alert notification is send to you if you are leaving a route that your are following.
This requires you to :ref:`record <sec-record>` your route. 
If you have loaded the route on the map that you want to follow and leave the trail (at minimum 25 m), then an alert notification with a sound will be send by Topo GPS, even
if the Topo GPS app is in the background. This is to warn you that you have left the route. This might be useful, because sometimes you might not realize that you are going in an unintend direction.
